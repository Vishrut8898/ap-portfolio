about me

Hi, I am a resident of INDIA residing in Mumbai city since 2010. I completed my diploma in Vfx and Animation course from Haridwar. I started my career as Roto Paint artist in 2010.

In the past years I have been furthering my experience on several different projects from television to feature films for the visual effects industry..
In my career, I have worked in multiple roles of the pipeline with diverse teams of artists under accelerated deadlines. Furthermore, my key specialties include Chroma keying, 2D composting, CG and live plate compositing, FX comp 2D Paint, Prep, Roto.

Worked on Projects like..
Sin City: A Dame to Kill, Deadpool 2, Pacific Rim Uprising, Justice league, Assassin's Creed, The King Historical, Wonder Woman 84, Dune, Venom 2, The Matrix Resurrections, Furiosa etc.
